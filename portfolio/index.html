Portfolio


Though finding love in art through painting, for the last few years my hands have decided to work in the medium of clay. I find the possibilities with this medium endless and the building process therapeutic. Clay forces you to pay attention, it becomes just you and the mud in constant conversation. Though interested in expressing my ideas and certain concepts through sculpture, I would also like to refine my throwing skills to combine sculpting with function. In my completed artworks I hope to explore our connection to the natural world in which we are participants, pose questions to our existence, as well as conceptualize happenings from my life in order to make sense or meaning of them. Through my process I find peace and purpose, and am given the opportunity to share with the world a creation which flowed out of my head and through my hands. .
